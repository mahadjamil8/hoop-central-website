Basketball Rules

Basketball is easier to understand when you know the basic rules. This page explains scoring, dribbling, fouls, and violations.

Basic Rules of Basketball

Scoring

A free throw is worth 1 point, a regular basket is worth 2 points, and a shot behind the three-point line is worth 3 points.

Dribbling

A player must dribble the ball while moving. If they stop dribbling and start again, that is called a double dribble.

Fouls

A foul happens when a player makes illegal contact, such as pushing, holding, or hitting another player while defending.

Traveling

Traveling happens when a player takes too many steps without dribbling. It gives the ball to the other team.
