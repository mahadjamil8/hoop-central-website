What Makes a Great Team?

Teamwork

A strong basketball team shares the ball, communicates, and trusts each player to do their job.

Defense

Defense helps teams stop easy points, create turnovers, and control the pace of the game.

Leadership

Great teams usually have leaders who keep everyone focused, calm, and ready during pressure moments.
