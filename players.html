Top Basketball Players

Six legendary basketball players who changed the game with their talent, leadership, scoring, and impact.

My Top 6 Players

Kobe Bryant

Kobe Bryant was known for his scoring, footwork, confidence, and Mamba Mentality. He became one of the most respected competitors in basketball history.

LeBron James

LeBron James is known for his strength, passing, scoring, leadership, and all-around game. He can control a game in many different ways.

Stephen Curry

Stephen Curry changed basketball with his shooting range, ball handling, and quick release. He made the three-point shot a major part of modern basketball.

Kevin Durant

Kevin Durant is one of the best scorers because of his height, shooting, and smooth offensive game. He can score from almost anywhere on the court.

Larry Bird

Larry Bird was known for his shooting, passing, toughness, and basketball IQ. He was one of the greatest forwards and competitors of all time.

Shaq

Shaq was one of the most dominant big men ever. His size, power, rebounding, and finishing made him almost impossible to stop near the basket.
